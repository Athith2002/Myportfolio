Capstone project

Project Summary:

The mobile app, tentatively named "Photography Pro," aims to serve both professional and beginner photographers by providing a comprehensive platform for editing, portfolio showcasing, tutorials, photography styles, and drone guides. The app targets individuals seeking to enhance their photography skills, refine their editing techniques, showcase their portfolios, and explore various photography styles and drone usage.

Project Vision:

The vision for "Photography Pro" is to become the go-to mobile application for photographers of all skill levels, offering a user-friendly interface with robust features tailored to meet the diverse needs of the photography community. By providing a seamless experience for editing, portfolio management, tutorial access, exploration of photography styles, and drone guidance, the app aims to empower users to elevate their photography skills and achieve their creative aspirations.

Project/Business Requirements:

  • Editing Section: Implement a feature-rich editing toolset offering a wide range of editing options, filters, and effects to enhance photographs.
  • Portfolio Section: Develop a portfolio management system allowing users to showcase their best works, organize their portfolios, and share them with the community.
  • Tutorial Section: Curate a library of tutorials covering various photography techniques, editing tips, and drone operation guidelines to support users in improving their skills.
  • Photography Style Exploration: Integrate a section dedicated to exploring different photography styles, providing insights, examples, and inspiration to users.
  • Drone Guide: Include a comprehensive guide for drone photography, covering regulations, best practices, and tips for capturing stunning aerial shots safely and legally.
